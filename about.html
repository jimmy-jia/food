About Us

Ever had trouble deciding where to eat?

So have we. As students at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign, we've had countless days where we are walking in the bone-chilling cold, thinking about where we want to grab lunch. Most times we walk down Green, looking left and right, unable to decide where to go. To solve this, we decided to create FoodSeeker to help solve this problem.
Please enjoy the fruits of our labors.
